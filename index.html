Installation

To play the game, you'll need to install Python 2.7 as well as the SFML Python bindings. Then, just hit https://github.com/inhumanundead/rlyeh/archive/master.zip to download R'lyeh. Just click on rlyeh.py to play

System Requirements

This game doesn't require much, just have a CPU that's better than a Pentium 2 and any of the modern operating systems (Windows 2000 through 8, Mac OS X, and most forms of Linux).
